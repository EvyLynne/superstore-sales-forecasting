Methodology & Caveats

Forecast uses Power BI's built-in exponential smoothing with 12-month seasonality and a 95% confidence interval. It assumes historical seasonality holds and includes no promotion or macro drivers. The interval widens with horizon — treat the band, not the line, as the answer. Q4 (Sep/Nov/Dec) consistently runs ~1.3–1.6× an average month.
